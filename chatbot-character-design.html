Orange

Chatbot Design

With numerous demands, services and client queries, there was a need for a virtual assistant to cater for the needs of consumers both on Messenger and WhatsApp. 

Iconic Cameroonian cultural codes came in handy.

The NW "toghu" and "ndop", the "keh’ ting" (Bamileke juju) ,
and the popular "motoboh" hairstyle for women.
The proposals were named after slangs, and Cameroonian lingua franca:
Sabitou (Know all), Tchitcha (chit-chat), Docta (Doctor), and Combi (My friend)

An animation was made to better visualize the character's behaviour and physiology
