Motion Design

Following a new brand refresh, the Group started marketing for its own range of solar products. As such the task was to communicate to the general public on digital.

With a limited budget and a tight deadline, doing a realistic 3D model of the solar products posed challenge since the delivery had to be done under 2 weeks, the recommendation was to make box-models of the products (2.5D) to easily accommodate debriefs and deliver in record time.
